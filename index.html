March – May

Spring — Prime Season

Rhododendrons blanketing the ridges in crimson and pink, crisp skies, and the clearest mountain views of the year. April is our most beloved month on the trail.

🍂
October – November

Autumn — Peak Season

Post-monsoon air brings razor-sharp visibility and golden light on the peaks. Dry trails, warm days, and cool starlit nights — the classic Nepal trekking window.

🌿
June – September

Monsoon — Raw & Lush

The trails come alive with roaring waterfalls, thick jungle greenery, and wildflowers you won't see any other time of year. Fewer trekkers, dramatic skies, and an intimacy with the forest that peak season simply can't offer. Best suited for experienced trekkers who don't mind wet boots.

❄️
December – February

Winter — Hidden Gem

December is one of our best-kept secrets. The crowds are gone, the air is crystal clear, and snow-dusted ridges make for extraordinary views. A quieter, more personal kind of trekking experience — and often our guides' personal favourite.
